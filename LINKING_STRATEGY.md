## Rules for Every New Page

1. Link TO the pillar page with varied anchor text (e.g., "complete managed IT guide", "managed IT services in York", "our full range of IT services")
2. Link TO 2-3 topically related cluster pages (not just /services.html)
3. Use varied anchor text — mix exact-match, partial-match, and natural phrases
4. After creating: update 2-3 EXISTING related pages to link back to the new page
5. Add to services.html Related Guides grid
6. Add to blog/index.html listing

## Link Equity Targets

High priority (should receive most internal links):
1. Pillar page (managed-it-services-guide-york-pa.html)
2. managed-it-services-york-pa.html
3. small-business-it-support-york-pa.html

## Anchor Text Variety Examples

Good:
- "managed IT services in York, PA"
- "local IT support for small businesses"
- "our managed IT plans"
- "York Computer IT management"
- "proactive IT monitoring"

Avoid (over-optimization):
- Same exact keyword every time
- Generic "click here" or "read more"
