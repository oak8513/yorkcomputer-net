What We Do For You

Every plan includes a bundle of services working together to keep your devices safe, fast, and backed up — all managed by us, all for one flat price.

💾

Data Backup & Recovery

Your files are automatically backed up on a schedule. If your computer crashes, gets stolen, or gets hit by ransomware, we can restore your data. Think of it like insurance for your files — you hope you never need it, but you'll be glad it's there.

🔓

Network Security Solutions

We harden your network against attacks — blocking unauthorized access, securing your Wi-Fi, and making sure the right devices can connect and the wrong ones can't.

📞

24/7 Technical Support

When something breaks at 8pm on a Friday, you shouldn't have to wait until Monday. Our support team is available around the clock for urgent issues so your downtime stays as short as possible.

🔍

Managed Detection & Response

MDR is like having a security guard watching your computer 24/7. It actively hunts for threats — viruses, hackers, suspicious behavior — and responds before damage is done. Way more powerful than basic antivirus.

🕵

Dark Web Monitoring

When companies get hacked, stolen passwords get sold on the "dark web" — a hidden part of the internet. We scan those places 24/7 for your email and business credentials. If you show up somewhere you shouldn't, we alert you immediately so you can act fast.

🛡

Antivirus Solutions

We deploy and manage enterprise-grade antivirus — not the stuff you buy at Best Buy. It's centrally monitored so we know the moment a threat is detected on any of your devices, and we respond right away.

🛠

Application Control

We control which programs are allowed to run on your computers. This stops employees (or ransomware) from installing unauthorized software that could cause problems or create security holes.

📋

Asset Management

We keep a complete inventory of every device on your network — what it is, how old it is, what software is on it, and when it needs to be replaced. No more mystery computers or forgotten old laptops creating security risks.

🚨

Anti-Ransomware Protection

Ransomware locks your files and demands money to unlock them. It's one of the most common attacks on small businesses. Our anti-ransomware layer watches for those patterns specifically and shuts them down before your files get encrypted.

🔒

Password Vault

A secure place to store all your passwords — business logins, banking, software, email. Everyone on your team gets access to what they need, and we can remove access instantly when someone leaves. No more sticky notes or "same password everywhere."

🔧

Proactive Maintenance

Windows updates, driver updates, software patches, disk cleanups, startup optimization — we handle all of it automatically in the background. Your computers stay fast, stable, and protected without you having to think about it or click anything.
