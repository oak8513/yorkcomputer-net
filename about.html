Who We Are

York Computer is a managed IT service provider based right here in York, PA. We work with small businesses and homeowners who need reliable technology support but don't want to hire a full-time IT person or deal with unpredictable hourly bills.

We're part of the same family as York Computer Repair — the local walk-in repair shop on the other side of town. Between the two, we cover everything from a broken laptop screen to full business cybersecurity management.

Our philosophy is simple: technology should make your life easier, not more stressful. Our job is to stay in the background, keep everything running, and only bother you when something actually needs your attention.
