What is a managed service provider (MSP)? +

A managed service provider (MSP) is a company that takes ongoing responsibility for your business IT — monitoring systems, handling security, managing updates, and providing support — all for a predictable flat monthly fee. It's like having a full IT department without the overhead of hiring one.

How is an MSP different from break-fix IT support? +

Break-fix IT means you pay for repairs after something goes wrong — unpredictable costs and downtime. An MSP like York Computer proactively monitors and maintains your systems to prevent problems in the first place. You pay a flat rate and get consistent, reliable IT management instead of emergency bills.
