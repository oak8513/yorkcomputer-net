Frequently Asked Questions

What is IT monitoring for small businesses? +

IT monitoring means a software agent on your computers sends health data to our platform around the clock. We watch for early warning signs — failing drives, memory pressure, missed backups, suspicious logins — and fix problems remotely before they turn into outages.

How does proactive monitoring prevent downtime? +

Many hardware and software failures give advance warning — a hard drive degrading over weeks, a Windows service crashing and restarting, memory errors accumulating. Our monitoring catches these signals early so we can replace the drive or fix the service before everything stops working.

Will monitoring software slow down my computers? +

No. The monitoring agent uses a tiny fraction of system resources — typically less than 1% CPU and under 50 MB of RAM. You will not notice it is there, but we will notice the moment something is not right.

Do you monitor servers and workstations, or just PCs? +

We monitor Windows and Mac workstations, Windows servers, and network devices like routers and switches. If it is on your business network and it is critical to your operations, we can monitor it.
