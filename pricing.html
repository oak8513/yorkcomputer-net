Pricing Questions

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts required. You can cancel anytime. We earn your business every month by doing good work.

Is this per computer or per location?

Plans are per device. Call us and we'll give you a bundled quote if you have multiple computers — we work with businesses of all sizes.

Do you charge extra for support calls?

No. Support is included in your monthly fee. Call or email us whenever you need help — that's what the plan is for.

What if my computer breaks and needs a repair?

Physical repairs are handled by our sister shop, York Computer Repair. They offer a flat $39.99 diagnostic fee and straightforward pricing.
